About KarigariHub

KarigariHub is a unique online marketplace that connects talented artisans with art enthusiasts from around the world. Our mission is to preserve and promote traditional craftsmanship while providing artisans with a global platform to showcase and sell their handcrafted creations.

Founded in 2020, KarigariHub has grown into a thriving community of over 1,000 artisans from various cultural backgrounds, offering a wide range of handmade products including pottery, textiles, jewelry, and home decor items.

Our Vision

At KarigariHub, we envision a world where traditional craftsmanship is valued and celebrated. We strive to create a sustainable ecosystem that empowers artisans, preserves cultural heritage, and brings unique, high-quality handcrafted products to conscious consumers worldwide.
